# VelumX Pitch Deck

## Slide 1: Title
**VelumX**
Gasless Transaction Infrastructure for the Stacks Ecosystem.
*Empowering the Next Million Bitcoin Users.*

## Slide 2: The Problem
**Users can't use dApps without STX.**
*   Friction: New users must go to an exchange, KYC, buy STX, and transfer it before their first transaction.
*   Complexity: Managing gas fees is the #1 reason for transaction abandonment.
*   Barrier: DeFi adoption on Stacks is capped by onboarding complexity.

## Slide 3: The Solution
**Gasless. Seamless. Native.**
*   **Paymaster Model**: Pay gas fees in USDCx or other stablecoins.
*   **Zero-Fee Tx**: Developers can sponsor 100% of user fees.
*   **One-Line Integration**: A powerful SDK that abstracts away the complexity of SIP-018 and relayer logic.

## Slide 4: Market Opportunity
**The Rise of Bitcoin L2s.**
*   Stacks is the leading Bitcoin L2.
*   Total Value Locked (TVL) is growing, but user experience lags behind.
*   VelumX is the essential infrastructure layer to bridge the Web2-to-Web3 gap.

## Slide 5: How it Works
**Native Sponsorship Architecture.**
1.  **User Signs Intention**: Partially signed transaction payload.
2.  **Relayer Logic**: VelumX calculates the STX fee and adds its signature.
3.  **On-Chain Execution**: The Stacks network processes the sponsored transaction.
4.  **Flexible Reimbursement**: Paymaster contract ensures the relayer is reimbursed in USDCx.
