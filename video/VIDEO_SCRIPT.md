# VelumX Demo Video Script & Storyboard

**Duration**: ~90 Seconds  
**Tone**: High-energy, professional, futuristic.  
**Aesthetic**: Dark mode, minimalist UI, animated graphs.

---

## Scene 1: The Problem (0:00 - 0:15)
**Visual**: A dark screen with a Bitcoin symbol. Slowly, a "Transaction Failed" popup appears in a generic wallet UI. Text overlay: "Where is my gas?"
**Narrator**: "You’ve finally found the perfect DeFi opportunity on Stacks. You’re ready to bridge, ready to swap... but there’s one problem. You don't have enough STX for the fee."
**Audio**: Atmospheric, slightly tense synth pad.

## Scene 2: The Solution (0:15 - 0:35)
**Visual**: Transition to VelumX logo. Neon orange and blue glows. A "sponsored" badge appears over a transaction card.
**Narrator**: "Meet VelumX. The gasless infrastructure that removes the friction from Bitcoin’s Layer 2. No more STX hurdles. No more KYC for a few cents of gas."
**Audio**: Transition to a driving, rhythmic electronic beat.

## Scene 3: The Paymaster Model (0:35 - 0:55)
**Visual**: Screen recording of the VelumX Swap interface. User selects "Pay in USDCx". The transaction confirms instantly.
**Narrator**: "With our native Paymaster protocol, you pay fees in the tokens you already have. Swap, bridge, and earn... all using USDCx. Seamlessly."
**Audio**: Satisfying haptic SFX on clicks.

## Scene 4: SDK for Developers (0:55 - 1:15)
**Visual**: Code snippet of `@velumx/sdk` being imported. A developer dashboard showing 99% conversion rates.
**Narrator**: "For developers, it’s a game-changer. One-line integration to offer 100% sponsored transactions. Keep your users in your app, not at an exchange."
**Audio**: Upbeat, tech-focused melody.

## Scene 5: Momentum (1:15 - 1:25)
**Visual**: Rapid montage of transaction logs, 150+ successful txs, and the "Production Ready" status.
**Narrator**: "150 transactions and counting. 98.5% success rate. The future of Stacks is gasless."
**Audio**: Building intensity.

## Scene 6: Call to Action (1:25 - 1:30)
**Visual**: VelumX logo, URL: `velumx.vercel.app`. Discord: `discord.gg/polimaf`. Twitter: `@leprofcode`.
**Narrator**: "VelumX. Empowering the next million Bitcoin users. Get started today."
**Audio**: Final resonant chord.
